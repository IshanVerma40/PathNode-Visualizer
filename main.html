Getting Started

  • Drag the Green (Start) and Red (End) nodes to reposition them.
  • Click and Drag over empty cells to create walls.
  • Select an algorithm from the top menu and hit "Visualize!".

Dijkstra's Algorithm

The father of pathfinding. It guarantees the shortest path by exploring all possible directions equally. Weighted edges are its specialty.

A* (A-Star)

A smart version of Dijkstra's that uses heuristics to "guess" the distance to the target, making it much faster.
